About Us

Numerous ongoing projects showcase an interdisciplinary nature, reflecting BITS Pilani's enduring support and encouragement for cross-disciplinary research over the years Both in terms of projects and intellectual output (publications and patents) Several Schemes have been launched.

SIRE Vision

Propel BITS Pilani to the forefront of cutting-edge interdisciplinary research and innovation globally

SIRE Mission

To address complex societal and industrial challenges by bringing together experts from diverse fields leading to best-in-class intellectual outputs with entrepreneurial acumen

SIRE Objectives

To nurture a vibrant and inclusive research ecosystem To develop futuristic interdisciplinary academic program To promote research across different departments / disciplines, focusing on PhD program To provide seed funds to such teams and support researchers To create a bouquet of Intellectual Output To collaborate with external Institutes and Industries To augment entrepreneurial landscape of BITS Pilani
